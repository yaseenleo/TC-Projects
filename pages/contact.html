We Want to hear from you

Get in touch with our team

Dua House was founded on the belief that addiction is not something to be ashamed of and that addicts deserve to get help. Our team of addiction specialists is dedicated to helping people get treatment and take their lives back from addiction.

Regardless of what kind of addiction you’re facing, there are many resources available to help you overcome it. From residential and outpatient treatment to ongoing therapies, the road to recovery is paved with help and with hope.

important reasons to contact us

You should contact us if:

  • You think you are struggling with an addiction
  • Someone you love has exhibited signs of addiction
  • You have tried unsuccessfully to overcome your addiction
  • You want to know what options are available to help you

Whether you or someone you love is struggling with an addiction to drugs or alcohol, there’s never been a better time to get help. If you believe someone you love is facing an addiction, we can help you understand what they’re going through. We can also help you stage an intervention or connect them with a recovery center.

Take the first step toward your new life by getting in touch with one of our addiction specialists.

About Us

Established in 2007 by Directors having more than 20 years of experience in working with Addiction, Dua House is a residential rehabilitation Facility which treats people suffering from alcohol and drug addictions. we recognize that addiction treatment has to go further than just rehabilitation into full resettlement and reintegration into work and society.
