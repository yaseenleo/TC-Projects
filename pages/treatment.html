Am I an Addict?

The Modified CAGE Test for All Addictions

This simple test is surprisingly accurate. Answer yes or no to each question

  1. Have you ever thought you should Cut down your drug or alcohol use?
  2. Have you ever felt Annoyed when people have commented on your use?
  3. Have you ever felt Guilty or badly about your use?
  4. Have you ever used drugs or alcohol to Ease withdrawal symptoms, or to avoid feeling low after using?

Your score: Score one point for each yes answer:

  • If you scored 1, there is an 75% chance you’re addicted to alcohol/Drugs.
  • If you scored 2, there is an 85% chance you’re addicted to alcohol/Drugs.
  • If you scored 3, there is a 99% chance you’re addicted to alcohol/Drugs.
  • If you scored 4, there is a 100% chance you’re addicted to alcohol/Drugs.

Addiction involves both physical and psychological dependence. To say people are psychologically addicted means they experience cravings, and they find it hard to imagine how they could cope without the drug. Physical addiction occurs because the body needs to adapt to the substance abuse – it does this by developing tolerance. Once people become physically addicted, it means they are going to experience withdrawal substances when they stop using the substance.

What We Treat

Our Approach to Treating Addiction & Co-Occurring Disorders

Our approach to addiction treatment is rooted in therapies that have been scientifically proven to help our clients achieve and maintain addiction-free lives. We take an integrated approach that addresses addiction as well as any mental health challenges they may be experiencing. Our holistic approach that help heal the whole person, mind, body and spirit, gives them very best chance at recovery.

Our core program includes medically monitored detox, therapies such as Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, Dialectical Behavior Therapy, Motivational Enhancement Therapy, Twelve Steps Facilitation, The CENAPS Model of Relapse Prevention Therapy, Group and Individual Counseling, Dual Disorder Recovery Counseling ,Life Skill Based Education, Psychodrama, Family Therapy, Continuing Care, Hypnosis and other complementary therapies.
