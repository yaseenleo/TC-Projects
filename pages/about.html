About Us

About Dua House

Established in 2007 by Directors having more than 20 years of experience in working with Addiction, Dua House way is a residential rehabilitation Facility which treats people suffering from alcohol and drug addictions. we recognize that addiction treatment has to go further than just rehabilitation into full resettlement and reintegration into work and society. Dua House way is one of the PAKISTAN’s most established and respected addiction rehab centre. Hundreds of people from all over the country turned to Dua House way for quality treatment, extensive services & therapies and dedicated family support.

Alongside our expertise, the passion of our team members who invest personally in each and every one of our patients’ recovery, creates the perfect atmosphere of understanding and warmth in which recovery can flourish. At Dua House, we will never give up on a patient, even if that patient has given up on themselves. Our enduring belief is that there is always a way into recovery from these problems, and it is our job to help you discover how.

Our detoxification, primary, secondary and aftercare treatment programmes are designed and delivered by those with rich experience of working with addictions and related disorders.

Why choose Dua House Ways

Our Complete Recovery Journey – from your initial enquiry, all the way through treatment and beyond into education, work, resettlement into the community and reengaging with family,Dua House is there to guide and support you.

Our Recognision

That overcoming addiction goes much further than kicking a habit; attitude and behaviours need to be challenged in order to achieve a successful and lasting recovery

Our Commitment

At Dua House, we strongly believe that everyone has the right to be given the opportunity to free themselves from the grip of addiction, and to be empowered through appropriate support to take control of their lives.

Dr Aamer Hafeez
